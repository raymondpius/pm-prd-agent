# AI Product Manager Copilot

> Turn raw feature ideas into structured PRDs, metrics frameworks, experiment plans, and product insights — instantly, in the browser.

**[🚀 Live Demo](https://raymondpius.github.io/ai-pm-copilot)**

---

## What It Does

Enter any feature idea or problem statement and the copilot generates a full product thinking framework across 6 sections:

| Section | Output |
|---|---|
| **Clarifying Questions** | 3–5 targeted questions on users, constraints, and business goals + key assumptions |
| **PRD** | Full Product Requirements Document with user stories, acceptance criteria, risks, dependencies, scope, prioritization (RICE + MoSCoW), and rollout plan |
| **Personas** | Detailed user personas with goals, pain points, and behavioral patterns |
| **Metrics Brain** | North Star metric, leading & lagging indicators, measurement risks, and instrumentation guide |
| **Experiment Plan** | Hypotheses, A/B test designs, control vs. variant, sample sizes, and expected outcomes |
| **Product Insights** | Failure modes, tradeoffs, alternative solutions, what NOT to build, and feature critique |

---

## How to Use

1. **Enter your idea** — type a feature idea or problem statement into the input box
2. **Choose a tone** — select Startup, Enterprise, or Technical to match your context
3. **Click Generate** — get a full PM framework in seconds; edit, copy, or export any section
